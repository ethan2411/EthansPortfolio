Exploring Sydney Airbnbs

Data 601 Term Project
Conducted extensive data analysis of a dataset containing over 24,000 Airbnb listings in Sydney, Australia.
Utilized Python and various data analysis libraries, such as pandas, geopandas, matplotlib, and plotly, for data cleaning, visualization, and exploration.
Implemented geospatial analysis to calculate distances from Airbnb neighborhoods to Sydney's top tourist attractions, providing valuable insights for travelers.
Presented findings in a comprehensive project report, including data visualization and meaningful insights for prospective Airbnb users.
Addressed key questions such as popular rental areas, common room types, frequently listed amenities, price variations, and the impact of reviews on Airbnb listings.

Exploration of Airbnb Price

Data 602 Term Project
Focused on understanding the factors influencing rental prices by investigating the impact of neighborhood, room type, and various property features.
Utilized statistical techniques such as ANOVA testing, Fisher LSD tests, and linear regression models to uncover significant differences and develop a predictive pricing model.
Found that most Sydney neighborhoods had significantly different mean prices for Airbnb rentals, providing valuable insights for travelers.
Developed a pricing prediction model using stepwise selection, achieving the best results in predicting Airbnb rental prices in Sydney, aiding both travelers and hosts in pricing their listings effectively.
