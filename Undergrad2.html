Analysis of Experimental Design

The project analyzes the effects of 8 different treatments on the decrease of sucrose solution. The data is loaded, reviewed and then analyzed using ANOVA.
The analysis includes diagnostic plots to check the assumptions of ANOVA, including normality of residuals and homoscedasticity. To satisfy the assumption of normality, a square-root transformation is applied to the data.
Finally, the project conducts a Fisher LSD test to determine if there are significant differences between the means of each treatment.
