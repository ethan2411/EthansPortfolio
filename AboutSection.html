About Me

My goal is to become a data scientist or data analyst, leveraging my analytical skills and knowledge of statistics to help organizations make data-driven decisions.

As I approach graduation, I am excited to explore opportunities in data science and analytics, where I can apply my skills to drive business outcomes and make a meaningful impact.
